1.  Executive  Summary  
Mirror  creates  a  digital  communication  profile  of  a  user  and  generates  text  and  voice  outputs  
that
 
resemble
 
the
 
user's
 
communication
 
style.
 
The  system  learns  from:  
●  Emails  ●  Messages  ●  Documents  ●  Transcripts  ●  Voice  recordings  
Mirror  does  not  simply  imitate  vocabulary.  
It  attempts  to  model:  
●  Tone  ●  Formality  ●  Directness  ●  Humor  ●  Sentence  structure  ●  Communication  habits  
●  Speaking  patterns  
The  goal  is:  
"If  someone  receives  the  generated  message,  they  should  believe  the  user  wrote  or  spoke  
it."
 
 
2.  Problem  Statement  
People  communicate  in  unique  ways.  
Current  AI  assistants  generate  generic  responses.  
Problems:  
●  Messages  do  not  sound  like  the  user  ●  Brand  voice  becomes  inconsistent  ●  Executives  spend  time  editing  AI  output  ●  Teams  struggle  to  maintain  communication  style  
Users  want  AI-generated  content  that  sounds  authentically  like  them.  
 
3.  Product  Vision  
Create  a  personalized  AI  communication  engine  capable  of:  
●  Learning  a  person's  style  ●  Replicating  their  writing  ●  Replicating  their  speaking  style  ●  Measuring  similarity  objectively  
The  system  should  answer:  
"How  would  I  say  this?"  
instead  of:  
"How  would  AI  say  this?"  
 
4.  Goals  
Primary  Goals  
Allow  users  to:  
●  Create  style  profiles  ●  Generate  text  in  their  style  ●  Generate  voice  responses  in  their  style  ●  Evaluate  similarity  
 
Secondary  Goals  
Provide:  
●  Style  analytics  ●  Communication  insights  ●  Persona  comparison  tools  
 
5.  Non  Goals  
The  MVP  will  NOT  include:  
●  Real-time  voice  calls  ●  Video  generation  ●  Face  cloning  ●  Social  media  automation  ●  Autonomous  posting  ●  Team  personas  
 
6.  Target  Users  
Content  Creators  
Need  consistent  voice.  
 
Executives  
Need  personalized  communication.  
 
Founders  
Need  scalable  communication.  
 
Professionals  
Want  AI-generated  content  that  sounds  authentic.
